About Us

About
LANCING TUBE MANUFACTURING CO.

The Company is Located at ADDA Industrial Area Durgapur (West Bengal, India). The Factory is situated over a well spread out area of about 6,500 sq. meters and the factory shed is about 18,000 sq. Ft. The company has adequate & uninterrupted Power & Water supply which is provided by Durgapur Projects Limited. The Company also boasts of a highly competent technical team of a highly Professional Engineers & Supervisors who are managing the factory not to mention the dedicated team of Commercial managers who are managing the office.

Lancing Tubes Manufacturing Co. was born as a Market Leader in the field of Oxygen Lancing Tubes. The Company continues the rich heritage which it had inherited. This position of supremacy is been maintained by its insistence on Quality Consciousness since its inception and with the help of the state of the art automatic Tube mill that is used to manufacture the tubes. Today, despite its tender age Lancing Tube Manufacturing Co. is one of the most popular names in the Oxygen lancing pipe industry. We not only cater to customers in West Bengal but our Customers spread accross the length & breadth of the country, i.e., Tamilnadu in the South to Kashmir in the North & Gujrat in the West to Arunachal in the North-East. We are also Exporting our Lancing Tubes to Africa and Bhutan . Lancing Tube Manufacturing Co. is a forward looking company pinning faith on advanced market planning as a major strategy for growth. Quality upgradation and innovation form a continuous process at the Company and this has won for us several high-profile coustomers in India and abroad. The Company believes in the policy of being highly responsive to customer's needs through a process of continuous review and evalution. Quality is assured with a full judged laboratory & continuous checks during Production.

Expansion and consolidation are the company's well defined corporate Philosophy. In Keeping with its avowed philosophy and policy of growth, Lancing Tube Manufacturing Co. has been consistently expanding and consolidating both in the existing and new Frontiers. The Company believes in supplying Quality Products in time at the cheapest prices paying the highest possible wages.
