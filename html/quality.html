Quality

Quality Assurance
The company believes in the principal of best possible Quality at the cheapest possible Price.As such we try to keep high standards of Quality at all spheres.

Raw materials are taken from quality suppliers and randomly tested for chemical composition.

Pipes are randomly batch tested for physical parameters, such as mass, diameter, length & surface finish.

Each & every pipe is subject A to hydraulic test & Random samples are tested at 750 PSI for long durations.

Random Samples are drawn and are subjected to bend test at 90° & 180° in cold conditions

Random sample are also subjected to Tensile & elongation tests.

Expansion test for sockets are also carried out.
