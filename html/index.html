LANCING TUBE MANUFACTURING CO.

Leading Manufacturer and Supplier of Oxygen Lancing Tubes and Pipes

Located in ADDA Industrial Area Durgapur, West Bengal, India, Lancing Tubes Manufacturing Co. operates from a spacious 6,500 sq. meter area with a 18,000 sq. ft. factory shed, enjoying uninterrupted power and water supply from Durgapur Projects Limited. Boasting a proficient team of engineers, supervisors, and commercial managers, we excel in producing Oxygen Lancing Tubes, maintaining our position as a market leader through unwavering commitment to quality. Our advanced automatic Tube mill ensures superior products, earning us a stellar reputation nationwide, from Tamil Nadu to Kashmir and Gujarat to Arunachal, as well as international markets like Africa and Bhutan. With a focus on innovation and customer satisfaction, we prioritize quality assurance through rigorous laboratory testing and continuous production monitoring.
